About Me

I'm a technical writer at Autodesk, based in San Francisco, CA. I've mainly for security and compliance teams, helping them communicate with auditors and customers about how they safeguard data. More recently, I've switched into a developer documentation role, working with developers to produce good internal and customer documentation. I love writing because I get to learn new things every day. I also love teaching, and I think anyone can learn to write well. When I'm not writing the docs, I'm spending my time coaching my colleagues and developing a writing center.

I received my Masters in Professional Writing from Carnegie Mellon Univeristy. This education has given me a solid foundation in many disciplines, including design, rhetoric, and user research. I'm currently taking a coding bootcamp to improve my programming skills.

Outside of work, I play clarinet. I am a proud member of the San Francisco Lesbian/Gay Freedom Band, and I have a seat on their Board as director of membership. The Freedom Band is the first openly gay music organization in the world, and it's recently been named the Official Band of San Francisco. The group has provided me with a community and musicians and activists that I can call friends.
